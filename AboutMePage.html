About Me

Hi my name’s Max and I’m a digital artist and game developer from the United States! I’ve spent my life as a creative, drawing most of life, it was only a matter of time before I began programming. I like many game devs, grew up a gamer, wishing to be a part of the process. My favorites were always immersive sims, so I always ambitioned to make one of my own. Ever since I graduated from Full Sail University late last year, I’ve learned a great deal about development in UE5. With a growing need for a professional portfolio to prove my skills I’ve been teaching myself programming languages, concepts, and engines. I recently taught myself C#, obtaining a digital certification through Microsoft's collaboration with freecodecamp. Currently I feel confident programing in C++, C#, and Unreal blueprint. I hope to prove my confidence with many projects. One of which includes this website. I learned to program in css, and html, and plan to continually update and upgrade as time goes on and my skills grow.
